The challenge

Financial services communications are high-stakes, high-regulation, and high-volume — all at once.

01

Fraud alerts that arrive too late

Seconds matter in fraud prevention. When an SMS fails or queues behind a batch campaign, the window to protect the customer closes — and liability opens.

02

Authentication blocked by carrier filtering

2FA and OTP messages are increasingly filtered by carriers as spam. Failed verification means failed transactions, abandoned onboarding, and support queues that cost real money.

03

Regulatory notices without proof of delivery

FCA, PRA, and GDPR mandate that certain communications are delivered and evidenced. Without per-message audit trails, compliance is a hope rather than a guarantee.

04

Multiple divisions, zero coordination

Retail banking, wealth management, and insurance each run their own messaging tools. Customers get duplicated, brand standards are inconsistent, and nobody has the full picture.

How Kaptea solves it

Purpose-built for the messaging challenges financial services actually face.

The pain

"Our fraud alerts go through the same queue as marketing. When we send a batch campaign, authentication messages get delayed — and customers lose money."

Priority routing with cross-channel failover

Pronto separates transactional and marketing queues with independent routing priorities. When SMS delivery fails, the platform automatically cascades to WhatsApp or email — so the fraud alert always arrives.

  • Priority queue separation for transactional vs. campaign messages
  • Automatic SMS → WhatsApp → Email failover cascade
  • Real-time delivery diagnostics per message
  • Event streaming to PagerDuty or Slack for failed critical messages

Per-message audit trail with compliance evidence
