What counts as a closed loop

Two closure types are billed: explicit (the customer actively confirms) and inferred (satisfaction is clear from evidence in the thread). A loop that simply goes quiet is never billed: no evidence, no charge. We call this "implied" closure and we track it as a product signal for you, but never charge for it.

Carrier and messaging costs

The loop fee covers orchestration, governance, analytics, and managed service. If you bring your own Twilio, there is no secondary message charge from Kaptea at all. If we back in Twilio on your behalf, that carrier cost is passed through as a transparent line item.
