Implementation

Five phases. No surprises. Live in weeks, not months.

Week 1
1
Discovery

Understand your landscape

We map your current communications stack, channels, CRM integrations, compliance requirements, and messaging volumes. This phase surfaces every dependency before a line is configured.

  • Existing Twilio account audit and configuration review
  • CRM integration requirements (Salesforce, Dynamics, HubSpot, Zoho)
  • Channel inventory - which channels, what volumes, what SLAs
  • Compliance and governance requirements mapping
  • User roles, divisions, and access control design
Output: Implementation plan, integration spec, and go-live timeline
Weeks 2–3
2
Design

Configure your instance

Your dedicated Pronto instance is provisioned and configured to your exact requirements - channels, branding, governance rules, templates, and user roles. No shared infrastructure.

  • Dedicated instance provisioned on AWS or Azure (EU, UK or US residency)
  • Whitelabel branding applied (logo, colours, product name)
  • Template categories, approval workflows, and division segmentation
  • SSO configuration (Azure AD, Okta, OIDC/OAuth2)
  • Event streaming connectors configured (Slack, Kafka, PagerDuty, etc.)
Output: Configured instance ready for integration testing
Weeks 4–6
3
Build & Integrate

Connect your systems

We integrate Pronto with your CRM, connect your Twilio account tokens, configure bidirectional contact sync, and set up event-driven workflows. The connector framework handles the heavy lifting.

  • Twilio account token integration (your account, your costs)
  • CRM connector activation with OAuth 2.0 and bidirectional sync
  • Contact list import and suppression list migration
  • Workflow automation configuration (triggers, actions, conditions)
  • API and webhook setup for custom integrations
Output: Fully integrated instance in staging environment
Weeks 7–8
4
Test & Optimise

Validate everything works

End-to-end testing across all channels, load testing for surge scenarios, UAT with your team, and team onboarding. Nothing goes live until your team is confident.

  • Channel-by-channel delivery testing (SMS, WhatsApp, Email, Voice, RCS)
  • Failover and resilience testing under simulated load
  • CRM sync validation with real contact data
  • User acceptance testing with your operations and compliance teams
  • Team training - admin, agent, and governance workflows
Output: Signed-off test results, trained team, go-live approval
Phased rollout
5
Go-Live

Launch with confidence

Phased go-live with real traffic, monitored in real-time by our team. HyperCare support for the first 30 days - elevated SLAs, dedicated Slack channel, and daily check-ins until you're fully bedded in.

  • Controlled rollout - start with one channel or division, then expand
  • Real-time delivery monitoring during initial traffic ramp
  • 30-day HyperCare period with elevated response SLAs
  • Dedicated Slack or Teams channel with your implementation team
  • Weekly performance reviews during the first month
Output: Production environment live, HyperCare support active
After go-live

Named support. Guaranteed response. Full stack coverage.

Every Kaptea customer gets a dedicated technical account manager, guaranteed response times, and direct escalation - for your entire communications environment.
