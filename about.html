About Kaptea

Compliance-first communications, built by people who've done it before.

Kaptea is the company behind Pronto, the communications platform for regulated enterprises. We're a small team. The customers we serve are not. And the standard we hold ourselves to is set by the people who depend on the messages we deliver.

Our story

Why we started Kaptea

Over three decades, the founding team helped enterprises deploy complex technology, global networks, data centres, cloud services, and omnichannel customer engagement platforms, into some of the most regulated and most demanding environments in the world.

The pattern we kept seeing: organisations would invest heavily in systems and automation, then still struggle to create experiences that felt reliable, human, and trustworthy. The platforms were powerful. The deployments were expensive. The operations were fragile. And the customer, somewhere in the middle of all of it, kept getting let down.

So we built Kaptea to close that gap. Pronto is the platform we always wanted to deploy: compliance-first by design, dedicated infrastructure per customer, and operationally simple enough that one ops lead can run it without raising a ticket for every change. The principle behind it is straightforward: promises are the atomic unit of service. Every customer interaction is a promise made. Every delivery, every escalation, every audit log is a promise kept.

How we work

Three principles that decide everything else

These aren't aspirational. They're how the team actually operates, and they're the reason customers stay.

Clarity over complexity

The customer's problem is the team's problem. We reduce friction across every touchpoint and treat simplicity as a strength, not a compromise.

Outcome-driven

Every decision is measured against a real result for a real customer. Not against the report. Not against the dashboard. Against what actually changes for them.

Built on trust

Compliance is engineering, not paperwork. Audit trails, role-based access, encryption, residency, and resilience are baked into the product, not bolted on for a procurement form.
