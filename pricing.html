Twilio usage costs

Kaptea platform fees cover orchestration, governance, analytics, and managed service. Twilio message delivery costs - SMS, WhatsApp, Voice, and Email - are billed separately at your Twilio account rate. Kaptea can manage consolidated billing on request.

Message delivery capacity

Each plan includes a base message delivery capacity. Need higher volumes? We have transparent pricing tiers that scale with your business - talk to us about the right fit. Annual plans pool your total capacity across the year.

Implementation fees

Platform subscription fees do not include implementation. Complex deployments - IVR builds, CRM integrations, surge architecture - are scoped separately as fixed-price projects. Ask about our deployment packages starting from $10,000.

Commercial terms

  • Monthly plans: 60-day termination notice
  • Annual plans: 5% discount on monthly rate
  • Annual volume pools across the full contract year
  • 60-day pilot available on Premium - ask your account manager
  • All prices exclusive of applicable taxes
