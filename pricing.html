How a closed loop is counted

You are billed only when there is clear evidence a loop has closed, in one of two ways: explicit (the customer actively confirms) or inferred (satisfaction is clear from evidence in the thread). Where a conversation simply goes quiet, we treat it as implied closure: tracked as a product signal for you, and always free.

Carrier and messaging costs

Your Kaptea fee covers orchestration, governance, analytics and managed service. Bring your own Twilio and the message cost stays entirely yours, direct from the carrier. Where we provide Twilio on your behalf, that carrier cost is passed through at cost, on a transparent line item.

Pricing questions, answered

How do carrier and messaging costs work?

Bring your own Twilio and the message cost stays entirely yours, direct from the carrier. Where Kaptea provides Twilio on your behalf, that cost is passed through at cost on a transparent line item. Your Kaptea fee covers orchestration, governance, analytics and managed service.

Can we bring our own carrier and AI model?

Yes. Every plan supports your own telco carrier and your own governed AI model, on a dedicated single-tenant instance with EU, UK or US data residency.

How is a plan scoped and priced?

Plans start at €1,500 per month and are scoped to your channels and volume in a short conversation. Enterprise volume commitments carry a blended lower rate; implementation and custom integrations are scoped separately.

How quickly can we go live?

Your dedicated, single-tenant instance is configured on your Twilio and live in days. Kaptea handles the build, number setup and CRM integration, while your team configures the outcomes you need.
