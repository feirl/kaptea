The problem

Twilio is powerful. But power without control creates risk.

Most enterprises use Twilio for one or two channels, managed by engineering, with no governance layer, no failover, and no operational visibility. The result is fragmented messaging, compliance gaps, and a growing list of carrier issues nobody owns.

Multiple vendors, no single view

SMS through Twilio, email through SendGrid or Mailgun, WhatsApp via a third party. Each has its own dashboard, its own reporting, and its own failure modes.

Kaptea: one platform, all channels, one audit trail

Engineering builds it, then owns it forever

Custom integrations between Twilio and your CRM require ongoing maintenance. When the developer who built it leaves, so does the knowledge.

Kaptea: pre-built connectors, managed by us, no code debt

No governance for non-technical teams

Marketing wants to send campaigns, operations need alerts, compliance needs audit trails — but nobody has a tool that serves all three without engineering involvement.

Kaptea: self-service UI with role-based access and template controls

Compliance is a hope, not a guarantee

No per-message audit trail, no template approval workflows, no evidence of delivery for the regulator. Your compliance team can't prove what was sent, when, or to whom.

Kaptea: full audit trail, template governance, exportable compliance evidence
