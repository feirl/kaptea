Background

A contact centre that didn't exist yet

The Hot Box Sauna is one of Ireland's fastest-growing wellness brands, a network of sauna facilities expanding rapidly across the country. When Nicole Guildea joined as Head of Operations, the contact centre setup was in its infancy. Each site had a mobile phone. There was no centralised system, no visibility on call volumes, and no meaningful way to manage the customer experience at scale.

The Challenge

Zendesk Talk was abandoning 60% of calls

The team had moved to Zendesk Talk. It didn't work. Six in every ten customers who called simply couldn't get through. The IVR was a generic AI: impersonal and off-brand. There were no metrics to monitor.

Nicole had come from a much larger contact centre environment; she knew what good looked like. And she knew Twilio Flex from a previous role. The question was: who could implement it properly for a small, fast-moving startup?

Centralised multi-site voice system

The Solution

Twilio Flex, built right, with Zendesk and Shopify wired in

Nicole contacted Twilio, who provided a list of partners. Alan was the first person she spoke to. The relationship was instant.

Twilio Flex contact centre

A cloud-native contact centre platform, configured specifically for The Hot Box Sauna's multi-site operation, handling inbound calls, queue management and routing.

Custom branded IVR

Recorded by a team member customers already knew and recognised. The difference between an impersonal AI and a voice that reflects the brand.

Zendesk + Shopify integration

Robert wired both systems directly into Flex. When a customer calls, their booking history and previous tickets surface automatically; agents don't have to ask.

Fast, guided implementation

Despite the complexity, implementation was quick. The Kaptea team held daily calls through the process, resolving the phone number porting issue that had been left unresolved by previous management.

Results

A contact centre worth running

  • Call abandonment dropped from 60% to under 7%

    The single most important metric. Transformed within weeks of go-live.

  • Full operational visibility

    Queue depths, volumes, abandonment rates, agent performance: all now visible in real time. Something the team had never had before.

  • Customer experience genuinely improved

    From wondering whether they'd get through, to an IVR that sounds like the brand and routes correctly every time.

  • Two systems, one view

    Zendesk tickets and Shopify bookings both surface on incoming calls. Agents work faster, ask fewer questions, resolve issues faster.

  • Platform ready to scale

    Three to four new sites planned this year. The contact centre infrastructure is already built for what's coming.
