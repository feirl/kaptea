Background

A global trading platform with a regulatory compliance gap

TickMill is a globally regulated online trading broker operating across multiple jurisdictions. Like all firms in its sector, it is subject to strict requirements around the recording and retention of customer communications. Every call that touches a client account must be logged, matched and held for six years.

The problem was structural. TickMill was running inbound calls through CloudTalk and outbound calls through HubSpot Voice. Neither platform was purpose-built for compliance. Agents were managing multiple tabs, recordings were inconsistently matched to customer records in HubSpot, and supervisors had limited visibility across the team.

The Challenge

Eight requirements. One platform had to meet all of them.

Kaptea's discovery process mapped a precise set of compliance, integration and operational requirements. Every item on the list was non-negotiable.

100% inbound and outbound call recording
Automatic matching of recordings to HubSpot CRM records
Six-year storage and retrieval for regulatory audit
Agent wrap-up disposition to record call outcomes
HubSpot integration for email, WhatsApp and voice in one workspace
SSO for secure, frictionless agent login
Supervisor panel with live queue stats and agent performance
Multi-country outbound dialling with number selection by territory

The Solution

Twilio Flex: the only platform with the flexibility to meet every requirement

Kaptea replaced both CloudTalk and HubSpot Voice with a single Twilio Flex deployment, deeply integrated with HubSpot CRM. Unlike off-the-shelf contact centre platforms, Twilio Flex is programmable: Kaptea could build exactly what TickMill needed, not configure around what a vendor had already decided.

Custom agent panel

Inbound calls surface at the top of the Flex panel alongside live caller details and history. Pending tasks are ranked chronologically and by priority, with disposition syncing to and from HubSpot.

100% call recording

All calls are automatically recorded and matched to the correct HubSpot contact and deal record. Agents declare outcomes at wrap-up, creating a complete audit trail.

HubSpot deep integration

Email, WhatsApp and voice tasks all appear within the same Flex workspace. Agents see full CRM context without switching tabs, and every interaction logs back to HubSpot automatically.

Custom supervisor panel

Separate supervisor views by business unit and country. Kaptea built custom metrics, live queue stats and third-party data feeds via API, going well beyond the out-of-box Flex Insights dashboard.

Multi-country outbound

Agents can select the outbound number by country for each call, ensuring customers see a recognisable local number. Country-level call permissions are managed centrally.

SSO and access control

Single sign-on integration removes friction at login while maintaining security standards. Chrome extension deployment made the switch seamless for the agent team.

Results

Full regulatory compliance, a better agent experience and one platform to manage it all

The Twilio Flex deployment closed the compliance gap that had been an ongoing risk for TickMill's regulatory standing. More immediately, it made daily life simpler for agents and supervisors: one platform, complete information, no manual reconciliation.

  • 100% call recording compliance

    Every customer call is now recorded, matched to the correct account and stored to meet the six-year retention requirement.

  • Eliminated manual reconciliation

    Recordings and outcomes sync automatically to HubSpot, removing the manual effort agents previously spent matching calls to CRM records.

  • Unified agent workspace

    Voice, email and WhatsApp are managed from a single Flex panel. Agents no longer switch between CloudTalk, HubSpot Voice and a CRM tab.

  • Supervisor visibility

    Custom-built supervisor panels give team leads live queue data, agent performance metrics and cross-country visibility from one screen.

  • Scalable for new markets

    The multi-country architecture means new territories can be added without a platform rebuild: permissions, numbers and routing are all configurable by market.
