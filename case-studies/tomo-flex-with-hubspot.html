Background

A well-funded mortgage startup, racing to scale

Tomo Networks set out to make mortgage approval hassle-free for US homebuyers. With a $70 million Series A behind them, the bar was set: a customer-centric mortgage process, multi-channel from day one, scalable to keep pace with rapid market expansion across the United States.

The CTO's problem: spin up enterprise-grade contact-centre infrastructure quickly enough that it doesn't become the bottleneck. Voice, SMS, email, and web chat all live, all integrated with the CRM, all supporting an agent team that needed to grow.

The Challenge

Build it fast, build it right, and don't slow the company down

The conventional path was a long-cycle enterprise contact-centre rollout: scoping months, integration work in quarters, with a "year one" timeline. That was incompatible with Tomo's growth curve. The contact centre couldn't be the slowest piece of infrastructure in the business.

Tomo needed a partner who could move at startup speed, deliver across four channels in parallel, integrate with the existing CRM and IVR routing, and ship analytics good enough to coach a growing agent team in real time.

The Solution

Twilio Flex across four channels, in eight weeks

Kaptea designed and built Tomo's contact-centre platform on Twilio Flex, with end-to-end delivery in 8 weeks. Voice (inbound and outbound), interactive SMS, email, and web chat all live, all routed through the same agent desktop, all integrated with Tomo's CRM.

Twilio Flex Insights provided the analytics layer: customised dashboards built for Tomo's management team, calibrated to the metrics they actually needed to grow the team and improve the customer experience.

Twilio Flex design and build

End-to-end design and software development on Twilio Flex. The agent desktop, the routing logic, the channel orchestration, the workflow, all built specifically for Tomo's mortgage process and growth model.

Four channels in eight weeks

Voice, SMS, email, and web chat shipped together, integrated with the CRM and IVR routing. No phased rollout, no "we'll add SMS in Q2." Day-one parity with where mortgage customers actually are.

Twilio Flex Insights

Customised analytics dashboards built for the management team's real questions. Performance, agent activity, channel mix, response times. Real-time enough to act on, structured enough to coach against.

Enterprise service support

Project management, platform documentation, and ongoing enterprise service support throughout the build and beyond. The CTO got a delivered platform, not a hand-off and an invoice.

Results

From launch to a quarter of the US market

Within 10 months of launch, Tomo had scaled from initial customer interactions to thousands of daily conversations across four channels, supporting an agent team of 35 and reaching more than a quarter of the US market. The contact centre kept up, instead of holding things back.

  • Live in 8 weeks, contract to production

    Twilio Flex deployed end-to-end across four channels in eight weeks. The contact centre was live and supporting agents before most enterprise rollouts have finished requirements gathering.

  • 4 channels, 1 agent desktop

    Voice, SMS, email, and web chat all routed through the same Flex desktop, all integrated with the CRM. Agents work in one place; customers reach Tomo wherever they prefer.

  • 35 live agents, scaling on the platform

    The team grew on Flex without re-platforming. Capacity tracked demand, not the other way around. New agents added on the existing infrastructure with no rebuild.

  • Quarter of the US market reached

    Within 10 months of launch, Tomo's mortgage product was reaching over a quarter of the United States. The contact centre was the operational layer that made that geographic scale conversational.

  • Real-time analytics for a growing team

    Twilio Flex Insights dashboards customised to Tomo's management metrics. Performance and agent activity visible in real time. Coaching, capacity planning, and forecasting all run from the same data.
