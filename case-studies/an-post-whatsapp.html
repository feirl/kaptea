Background

A new channel, a complex environment

An Post is Ireland's national postal service and one of the country's largest semi-state organisations. Its digital customer engagement team had identified WhatsApp as the next natural channel, a way to meet customers where they already were. But WhatsApp Business API isn't a plug-in. Attempts to build the integration internally hit a wall: the technical complexity was well beyond what a non-technical team could configure on their own, and internal engineering resource was stretched elsewhere.

The Challenge

Making WhatsApp work inside a semi-state

Three requirements had to be satisfied simultaneously: technical integration of the WhatsApp Business API and Twilio routing, full GDPR and Irish data residency compliance, and navigation of semi-state procurement (multiple internal sign-off stages, compliance questionnaires, security reviews, and payment processes). There was no shortcut.

The Solution

Right partner, right time, backed by Meta

The introduction came through Twilio's partner network. Crucially, Meta agreed to sponsor a proof of concept sandbox, which transformed the conversation internally. It meant An Post could see what the solution would actually look like before committing budget. Because the engagement came via the existing Twilio partnership, it also bypassed the normal lengthy tender process, moving through procurement far more quickly than a cold supplier relationship would have allowed.

Meta-sponsored proof of concept

A fully-funded sandbox environment that brought the concept to life, giving An Post's stakeholders something tangible before procurement commitment.

Twilio-powered WhatsApp delivery

Built on Kaptea's existing Twilio partnership, giving An Post immediate access to enterprise-grade WhatsApp infrastructure without starting from scratch.

GDPR and data residency

Full compliance with Irish data protection requirements: all PII processed within Ireland, audit trail maintained throughout, and Meta platform compliance from day one.

High-standard MVP

Gillian had a clear vision: it had to be done right, or customers wouldn't adopt it. Kaptea delivered a high-end MVP that iterated rapidly into a production solution.

Results

From concept to production

Every requirement was met. Semi-state bureaucracy, Meta timelines and procurement complexity were navigated without compromising the quality of the solution. The result was a production-grade WhatsApp channel operated by a non-technical team.
