The problem

Enterprises don't have a channel problem. They have an orchestration problem.

📡

Channels managed in silos

SMS through one vendor, email through another, WhatsApp via a third. No unified view, no consistent governance, no cross-channel failover when one path goes down.

🔇

Messages disappear without trace

When a critical message fails, nobody knows until a customer complains. Delivery diagnostics are buried across multiple dashboards with no single source of truth.

🏗️

Integration takes months, not days

Connecting channels to your CRM, governance layer, and analytics requires custom engineering. Every new channel means another integration project.

How it works

One orchestration layer across every channel your customers use.

Five channels, one interface

Pronto connects SMS, WhatsApp, Voice, Email, and RCS through a single unified inbox. Agents see the full conversation history across channels — no switching between tools, no lost context.

  • Send bulk campaigns or 1-to-1 conversations across any channel
  • Two-way messaging with AI-assisted reply suggestions
  • WhatsApp template management with approval workflows
  • Click-to-call voice with call recording and queue management
  • HTML email campaigns with SendGrid delivery and open/click tracking
[Screenshot: unified inbox with SMS + WhatsApp
conversation for same contact]

Cross-channel failover that never drops a message

When SMS delivery fails, Pronto automatically routes through an alternative channel. Every message gets delivery confirmation, and every failure gets a diagnostic trail — so you know exactly what happened and why.

  • Automatic failover: SMS → WhatsApp → Email cascade
  • Real-time delivery diagnostics per message
  • Event streaming to Slack, Kafka, PagerDuty for instant alerts
  • Load-aware routing during high-volume surge events
[Screenshot: delivery diagnostics dashboard
showing failover cascade in action]

Inbound orchestration — route, assign, resolve

When customers reply or call in, Pronto routes conversations to the right agent based on channel, skill, and availability. Pending conversations queue in real-time, with Socket.io pushing updates to agent dashboards the moment a new interaction arrives.

  • Inbound routing across SMS, WhatsApp, Voice, and webchat
  • Real-time agent dashboard with pending conversation queue
  • Task assignment with history tracking and resolution logging
  • AI-generated contact summaries for instant context
  • Suppression list management with opt-out reason tracking
[Screenshot: agent dashboard showing
inbound conversation queue with routing indicators]

Outbound campaign orchestration built for scale

Design, schedule, and send multi-channel campaigns through a step-by-step wizard. Pronto batches large sends to maintain deliverability, tracks status in real-time, and gives you per-contact delivery visibility.

  • Campaign creation wizard with channel, audience, and content selection
  • Scheduled or immediate delivery with batch processing
  • Link shortening with click tracking for SMS
  • A/B testing across email, SMS, WhatsApp, and RCS templates
  • AI-powered analytics with anomaly detection and benchmarks

Template reuse guide To create another feature page from this template:
1. Update the breadcrumb, h1, hero-sub, and hero visual
2. Rewrite the 3 pain cards for the specific feature's problems
3. Replace the 3 how-it-works blocks with that feature's capabilities (pull from product specs)
4. Update the metrics strip with relevant data points
5. Swap the customer proof for one relevant to that feature
6. Update the 3 related capability cards to link to adjacent features
7. Update the closing CTA copy to reference the feature
