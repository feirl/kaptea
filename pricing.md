# Kaptea Pricing

> Kaptea Pronto is a dedicated-instance enterprise communications platform. Pricing starts from $1,500/month.

## Platform Fee

| Tier | Monthly Fee | Included Messages | Best For |
|------|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| Starter | From $1,500 / ~£1,200 | Up to 1 million | Single-country, single-team deployments |
| Growth | Custom | Multi-million | Multi-country or multi-division deployments |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Regulated enterprises with complex governance requirements |

All tiers include:
- Dedicated instance (no shared tenancy)
- All channels: SMS, RCS, WhatsApp, Voice, Email, Social
- Full governance and compliance features (RBAC, audit trail, template management)
- Implementation and onboarding support
- 99.5% message delivery SLA

## Carrier Usage

Carrier fees (Twilio, Telnyx) are billed separately at standard carrier rates and are not marked up by Kaptea. Customers either bring their own carrier account or Kaptea can help establish one.

Typical carrier costs for reference (2025):
- SMS (UK): approximately £0.04 per message
- SMS (US): approximately $0.0079 per message
- WhatsApp (conversation-based pricing): varies by country and category
- Voice (outbound UK): approximately £0.013 per minute

## What Is Not Included

- Carrier message costs (billed directly by Twilio or Telnyx)
- Custom development work outside standard configuration
- Data migration from legacy systems (quoted separately)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there a minimum contract term?**
Standard contracts are 12 months. Shorter pilots can be arranged for qualified enterprise buyers.

**Can we bring our own Twilio account?**
Yes. Pronto connects to your existing Twilio or Telnyx account. Your carrier rates and direct relationship remain yours.

**Do AI/Conversational AI features cost extra?**
Conversational AI (agent builder, conversation intelligence, sentiment analysis) is included in the platform fee. LLM inference costs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) are passed through at cost.

**Is there a free trial?**
Kaptea does not offer a self-serve free trial. We run scoped proof-of-concept deployments with qualified enterprise buyers. Book a demo to discuss.

**How quickly can we go live?**
Most customers are live within hours of contract signature. Dedicated instance provisioning takes hours, with configuration and testing layered on from there.
