The challenge

Most communications platforms stop at the API. Real journeys need logic, branching, and human checkpoints.

01

Every campaign is a one-off engineering ticket

Marketing wants a sequence. Operations wants an escalation. Product wants an event-driven journey. Each one ends up in a backlog because the platform offers an API, not a builder.

02

Logic lives in spreadsheets and Slack threads

The branching rules, approval flows, and exception handling for your customer journeys aren't in the comms platform. They're in scattered docs, tribal knowledge, and ad-hoc Zaps.

03

No memory across steps

The customer responded to the SMS, opened the email, ignored the call. Most stacks treat each touchpoint as a separate campaign. The next step has to look up state manually.

04

Compliance can't see the workflow

When a regulator asks why a customer received a particular message at a particular time, the answer lives in three systems and a custom integration. Auditing a journey takes days.

Human-in-the-loop with full audit

Approval steps pause a workflow and route the pending action to a named approver, role, or division. Approve, reject with reason, or edit before approving. Every decision is logged with user, timestamp, and outcome. Built for regulated comms, marketing template review, and AI handoff.

  • Approval gates per step, per branch, or per workflow
  • Routing by role, named approver, or division
  • Reject-with-reason and edit-then-approve flows
  • Per-step audit trail exportable as CSV
  • Useful for marketing approvals and human-in-the-loop AI
