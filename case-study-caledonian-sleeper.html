Background

A nighttime operator with a unique communications challenge

Caledonian Sleeper is the overnight passenger rail service connecting London with Edinburgh, Glasgow, Inverness, Aberdeen and Fort William. Unlike most train operators, it is reservation-only: over 91% of passengers have shared their contact details at the point of booking.

That means Kaptea holds communication data for nearly every person on board. When a service is delayed, cancelled or disrupted at 2am, the ability to reach all affected passengers quickly is not a nice-to-have. It is operationally essential.

The Challenge

Complex requirements, a small team and a system built for volume

The service is operated by Serco on behalf of Transport for Scotland. The operations and customer service teams are lean. They needed a platform that could handle high-volume burst messaging with minimal training overhead, while meeting strict data privacy and compliance standards.

Seamless SMS and email comms from one platform
Strict GDPR compliance and data privacy controls
SMS obfuscation to protect passenger mobile numbers
Role-based access for SuperAdmins, Advisors and Ambassadors
Rapid implementation of updates and custom flows
Detailed messaging analytics and reporting
Unlimited messaging templates for recurring service scenarios
Business continuity and 24/7 support for mission-critical use

The Solution

A custom Pronto build, shaped around how the team actually works

Kaptea built a bespoke Pronto instance for Caledonian Sleeper, integrating Twilio Messaging for SMS and Twilio SendGrid for email. The platform was designed around the service's specific operational roles, not a generic contact centre model.

Role-based access

Three distinct user roles: SuperAdmins, Advisors and Ambassadors, each with defined permissions. The right people can do the right things, nothing more.

SMS obfuscation

Passenger mobile numbers are masked within the platform, meeting Serco's strict privacy requirements and protecting personal data from internal misuse.

Email via SendGrid

Twilio SendGrid integration added a full email channel alongside SMS, allowing the team to send the right message through the right channel depending on urgency.

Unlimited templates

The team can create a message once and reuse it as a template across future incidents. Common service scenarios are covered from day one.

WhatsApp channel

Pronto now allows Caledonian Sleeper to pilot WhatsApp as a passenger communication channel, using the same template and analytics infrastructure as SMS and email.

24/7 Enterprise support

A dedicated Enterprise support agreement provides continuous monitoring, rapid issue resolution and change management for a service that operates around the clock.

Results

Faster passenger contact, higher reliability, new channels

The Pronto deployment gave the Caledonian Sleeper team the confidence to operate without engineering dependency. Messaging flows are managed by the operations team directly. Uptime has improved year-on-year, and WhatsApp has opened a new communication option for passengers who prefer it.

  • Mission-critical reliability

    Uptime is now consistently high. The team can recall no outages in the past year, a material improvement on prior experience.

  • Push-button passenger contact

    During night disruptions, the operations team can reach all affected passengers quickly, without waking engineers or waiting on external support.

  • GDPR and privacy compliance

    Full alignment with data protection standards, including SMS obfuscation, role-based access controls and GDPR-compliant data handling.

  • WhatsApp channel launched

    Caledonian Sleeper is now experimenting with WhatsApp as a third passenger communication channel, enabled by Pronto's multi-channel architecture.

  • Intuitive platform, low training overhead

    The interface is simple enough that new staff members get up to speed quickly, keeping operational risk low as team composition changes.
