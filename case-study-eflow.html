Background

National scale, small team, zero tolerance for error

eFlow operates Ireland's nationwide electronic toll network, a critical piece of public infrastructure. The marketing team is small, but its communications responsibilities aren't: T&C changes, billing migrations, website launches and service updates all need to reach a customer base that runs into the millions.

The team was already using Twilio for its contact centre and automated SMS. When the need for a proper email platform became urgent, Kaptea's existing relationship with Twilio made them the logical choice. The timing worked out well; the supplier relationship removed procurement friction and shortened the path from conversation to deployment.

The Challenge

Compliance first, then usability

eFlow's low risk appetite meant that security and compliance weren't negotiable. But the team also needed something a marketing manager could actually use, without hard-coding HTML templates or raising IT tickets every time a campaign needed to change. The requirements spanned governance, deliverability and day-to-day operational control.

The Solution

Campaign management and case operations: one platform

Pronto gave eFlow's marketing team two distinct capabilities in one platform: large-scale broadcast campaigns and targeted case management communications. Initially the designer tool felt a little basic, but when Pronto v1.5 arrived it was, in Darren's words, "a game changer", flexible enough that he found himself getting precise over desktop versus mobile layouts, icon placement and template composition, entirely within Pronto.

Broadcast campaigns at scale

From paperless billing migrations to website launches, Pronto handles sends to over one million customers with full deliverability controls and real-time analytics.

Case management email operations

Customer queries raised via the website are batched and responded to via Pronto. In the last four months this alone has significantly improved operational efficiency, replacing ad-hoc Outlook emails with properly formatted, consistent templates.

99% sender reputation

Kaptea manages SPF, DKIM and DMARC configuration and actively monitors eFlow's sender reputation. Robert is on hand for any deliverability questions and available to any member of the eFlow team directly.

Campaign analytics that go deep

Bounce rates, drops, spam reports, click-through data: all exportable. More granular than tools like MailChimp or Cheetah Mail, and Robert exports full monthly reports on request.

Results

A platform the team actually wants to use

eFlow's marketing team now runs national-scale email operations independently. The compliance baseline that was non-negotiable at the start has become a matter of course, and two new use cases have opened up that weren't part of the original brief.

  • National-scale campaigns without IT

    The marketing team sends to over a million customers independently. No engineering tickets. No waiting.

  • Case management transformed

    Switching from ad-hoc Outlook emails to Pronto-formatted templates improved both the customer experience and internal efficiency. The team now sends around 200 case response emails at a time.

  • 99% sender reputation maintained

    Consistent deliverability management by Kaptea means eFlow's domain reputation is protected as a matter of course, not something anyone has to think about.

  • Deep analytics on demand

    The team now tracks bounces, spam rates and click-throughs as standard, metrics most email platforms don't surface this clearly, and more than Darren had seen with previous tools.

  • WhatsApp expansion in view

    Darren has flagged to his manager that Kaptea's WhatsApp capability is available, positioning the platform for the next channel evolution as inbound contact options are reviewed internally.
