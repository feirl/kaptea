How critical conversations build trust
A promise made, and a promise kept.
Trust is not built through good intentions, marketing or technology. It is built through action: when someone says they will do something, they do it. Every customer conversation is one continuous loop through four stages. Carry a commitment cleanly through all four and the loop closes, trust compounds.
Trust
Score
1 · Listen
Hear what is really being asked
2 · Negotiate
Make the commitment explicit
3 · Execute
Get it done, keep them informed
4 · Confirm
Confirm they are satisfied

The metric that falls out of the flywheel is a live trust score, built from closed loops versus broken loops, and visible for every customer and every conversation.
