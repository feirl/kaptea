Background

A reservation platform that needs a voice for every restaurant

Quandoo operates one of the world's leading restaurant reservation platforms, connecting diners to thousands of restaurants across multiple markets. A core part of that experience is voice: diners calling to make reservations, or restaurants needing to speak to incoming callers.

Each restaurant is different. Some want specific routing logic. Some want particular options in the IVR flow. What works for one merchant doesn't always work for another. The platform's value depends on its ability to serve individual restaurant requirements at scale, not a one-size-fits-all IVR that treats every merchant identically.

The Challenge

Inflexible, expensive, and about to end

The existing IVR was built by a third party. The problem wasn't capability; it was flexibility. Every customisation, however minor, required going back to the original developer, scoping the change, and paying for it. When a restaurant wanted something specific, Quandoo couldn't just configure it themselves. They had to commission it.

And when the contract with that provider was ending, the window to move was tight. Quandoo needed a replacement that could be stood up quickly, without sacrificing the per-restaurant configurability that was essential to their model.

The Solution

Configure once, adapt always, through a Slack message

Kaptea was introduced by Twilio. The first conversations stood out immediately: a can-do attitude, clear technical knowledge, and an evident willingness to understand Quandoo's specific requirements rather than pushing a standard product. Where a previous provider had responded to every change request with cost and complexity, Kaptea's answer was simply: we'll find a way.

With the existing contract ending, Kaptea moved quickly to deliver an MVP. The harder challenge was extracting the requirements from Quandoo's own resource-constrained team, but Kaptea worked above and beyond to hit the deadline, getting the information they needed and building around it.
