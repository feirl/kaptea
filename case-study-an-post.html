Background

A high-stakes campaign with no room for error

An Post is responsible for delivering TV Licensing communications to households across Ireland: millions of emails in English and Irish (Gaeilge), carrying legally significant notices. The existing process was fragile. Bulk email tools with clunky interfaces, limited deliverability controls and no audit trail for compliance teams.

The organisation needed a platform it could own and operate. Not a black-box service they'd have to escalate to engineers every time something needed changing.

The Challenge

A list of requirements longer than most vendors could meet

Kaptea's discovery process uncovered a detailed set of requirements spanning security, governance, deliverability and usability. An Post needed everything to work together, out of the box.

Irish data residency for all PII
Full GDPR compliance with 90-day data retention
SPF, DKIM, DMARC and one-click unsubscribe
AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS in transit
Two-factor authentication via SMS and email
Role-based access controls with admin-only template editing
Bilingual templates (English & Irish)
Open-hours controls to prevent accidental out-of-hours sends
Dynamic personalisation (name, address, TV reference, expiry)
Bulk CSV upload with automatic bad-data removal
Backward compatibility with legacy internal systems
AWS-secured file storage for images and attachments

The Solution

Pronto: built for exactly this kind of complexity

Kaptea deployed a dedicated instance of Pronto, integrated with An Post's existing Twilio SendGrid account. The implementation began with a collaborative workshop to map requirements to the platform's governance and analytics structure. No assumptions, no generic templates.

Deliverability by design

Sender authentication (SPF, DKIM), DMARC policy, forward and reverse DNS, and low spam rate management, all configured from the start to protect An Post's domain reputation.

Enterprise security

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS in transit, role-based access controls, 2FA at login, and Irish data residency for all personally identifiable information.

Campaign governance

Open-hours controls, admin-only template editing, custom footers, preview and test-send functionality: the guardrails that turn a powerful tool into a safe one.

Operational self-service

Campaign managers can initiate, personalise and schedule campaigns without engineering support. Custom CSV upload handles bulk data with automatic validation.

Analytics that mean something

Real-time deliverability dashboards covering bounces, clicks and spam reports, with exportable analytics for compliance reporting and historic performance views.

Dynamic personalisation

Automatic population of customer name, address, TV reference number and expiry date. URL parameters dynamically mapped to customer IDs at send time.

Results

From pilot to long-term partnership

The initial deployment gave An Post's team immediate confidence: a platform they could operate, audit and trust. The pilot demonstrated capability across every requirement, leading directly to a long-term contract.

  • Improved campaign execution

    Simplified processes for initiating, personalising and scheduling at scale, without raising a ticket to IT.

  • Protected domain reputation

    Real-time analytics gave the team actionable feedback on deliverability, preventing the reputation damage that had been a risk with previous tooling.

  • Full regulatory compliance

    GDPR alignment from day one, with Irish data residency, 90-day retention policies and a full audit trail for compliance reporting.

  • Smooth adoption

    End-user documentation, staff training and end-to-end testing ensured the team were confident before go-live, not learning on the job.

  • Pilot converted to long-term contract

    The platform's performance across all requirements gave the procurement team the confidence to move to a long-term agreement.
