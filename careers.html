How we work

Promises made, promises kept.

The principles below aren't aspirational. They're how the team actually operates, and they're the reason customers stay.

The customer's problem is the team's problem

If a customer raises an issue, the team owns it until it's resolved. There is no handoff. There is no ticket queue. The fastest path between a problem and the person who can fix it is the path we take.

Compliance is engineering, not paperwork

Audit trails, role-based access, encryption, residency, and resilience are baked into the product. They aren't bolted on for the sake of a procurement form. We build for the regulator first because that's the bar our customers have to clear.

Small team, high autonomy

You'll have ownership of meaningful surface area from week one. Decisions happen close to the work. Approvals don't take five steps. We hire people who can hold the standard themselves.

We ship for the long run

Pronto is a platform customers stay on for years. We optimise for what's still working in five years, not what looks impressive in a quarterly all-hands.
