The problem

Enterprise SMS isn't a sending problem. It's a governance and visibility problem.

Deliverability gaps no one sees

Messages that fail silently create compliance exposure and erode customer trust. Without per-message diagnostics and automatic retry, you only find out when a customer complains — often too late for regulated communications.

Governance that doesn't scale

Bulk campaigns sent outside any approval workflow, templates drifting from compliance standards, opt-outs missed across disparate systems. For regulated organisations, this isn't just inefficiency — it's regulatory exposure.

Infrastructure that breaks at scale

A utility outage, a public health alert, a fraud wave — and your SMS infrastructure is the first thing to buckle. Without load-aware routing and surge capacity, the moments that matter most are the ones you can't deliver on.

RCS: the upgrade SMS deserves, without the risk

Rich Communication Services gives you branded sender identity, read receipts, interactive reply buttons, and rich media cards — all on the same carrier infrastructure as SMS. Kaptea sends RCS where the device and network support it, and falls back to SMS automatically when they don't. One send, best possible experience, zero manual management.

  • Verified brand identity

Compliance-first campaign management

Every campaign runs through a structured approval workflow before a single message is sent. Templates are version-controlled with change requests logged for audit. Opt-outs are captured automatically — a contact who replies STOP is suppressed instantly, across every future send, with reason and timestamp recorded. For regulated organisations, this isn't optional — it's built in by default.

  • Step-by-step campaign wizard with audience, content, and schedule stages
  • Template library with approval workflows and version history
  • Automatic opt-out capture and suppression list management
  • Character count and SMS segment tracking during composition
  • Automatic URL shortening with click-through tracking
  • A/B testing with configurable split percentages
  • Full audit log: who sent what, when, to whom, with what result

Full delivery visibility — per message, in real time

Kaptea tracks every message from queue to delivery. Real-time campaign dashboards show per-contact status — Queued, Sent, Delivered, Failed — with cost and failure reason visible at a glance. Common questions about SMS & RCS

SMS is the universal baseline — delivered to any mobile device globally. RCS is the next-generation standard: branded sender identity, read receipts, rich media cards, and interactive reply buttons. Kaptea sends RCS where supported and automatically falls back to SMS when the recipient device or network doesn't support it — so you always get the best experience without managing two separate sends.
Kaptea routes messages through carrier-grade infrastructure on Twilio and Telnyx, with intelligent load-aware routing and automatic retry logic. If a message fails, the platform diagnoses the failure and either retries or triggers cross-channel failover — routing to WhatsApp or Voice. Every failure produces a diagnostic trail so you know exactly what happened, not just a failed status code.
Kaptea maintains a built-in suppression list that is automatically updated when contacts reply STOP or any recognised opt-out keyword. Suppressed contacts are excluded from all future campaigns on that channel. Opt-out reasons and timestamps are logged in the audit trail. You can also manage suppression lists manually and export them for regulatory reporting.
