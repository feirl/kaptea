Background

A statutory body with strict communication obligations

The Teaching Council is the professional regulatory body for teaching in Ireland. It registers over 100,000 teachers and is responsible for setting and maintaining standards across initial teacher education, fitness to teach and ongoing professional development.

Its communications are not marketing. They are regulatory notices: registration renewal reminders, fitness-to-teach notifications and time-sensitive updates that teachers are legally required to receive. The Council needed a platform capable of delivering high-volume bulk SMS and email to a large, distributed audience, with the governance and data standards a public sector body is required to meet.

The Challenge

Public sector procurement with no room to compromise

The Teaching Council ran a formal competitive tender for bulk SMS and email messaging services. The requirements reflected the obligations of a statutory body handling sensitive personal data for over 100,000 registered professionals.

The Solution

Pronto: procurement-grade compliance, without enterprise complexity

Kaptea responded to the Teaching Council's formal competitive tender for bulk SMS and email messaging services. Pronto was selected on the strength of its compliance architecture, data residency guarantees and the team's ability to configure the platform around the Council's specific operational requirements.

Irish data residency

All teacher data is stored within Ireland, meeting the Council's requirements as a public sector body handling sensitive personal information at scale.

GDPR by design

90-day data retention policies, role-based access controls and full audit trails provide the governance layer the Council's compliance team requires.

Bulk SMS and email

A single platform for both channels, with scheduling, template management and list upload, allowing the comms team to plan and execute campaigns without technical support.

Secure data handling

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS in transit and strict access controls ensure that teacher PII is protected from ingestion to deletion.

Admin-only controls

Campaign creation and template editing are restricted to authorised administrators, preventing accidental or unauthorised sends from a platform handling regulatory communications.

Delivery analytics

Real-time reporting on delivery rates, bounces and opt-outs gives the team confidence that registration communications are reaching their audience.

Results

Regulatory communications at scale, with public sector confidence

The Pronto deployment gave the Teaching Council a platform it could stand behind in a procurement audit, operate without engineering dependency and trust to deliver to a large, distributed audience reliably.

  • Procurement compliance

    Pronto was selected through a formal competitive tender process, meeting every requirement the Council specified for bulk messaging in the public sector.

  • GDPR and data residency assured

    All personally identifiable data is stored in Ireland, with documented retention policies and access controls to satisfy the Council's data protection obligations.

  • Operational self-service

    The comms team can schedule and send bulk SMS and email without raising requests to IT, reducing lead times on time-sensitive regulatory communications.

  • Delivery confidence at scale

    Real-time analytics give the team visibility over delivery rates, bounce categories and opt-outs across large send volumes.

  • Governed access

    Role-based controls ensure that only authorised administrators can create campaigns or edit templates, keeping regulatory communications under strict governance.
